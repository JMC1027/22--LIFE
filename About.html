Who We Are

22 - LIFE is an online platform for active duty, and veterans to share, connect, and support one another in an unfiltered, uncensored, military style online enviorment. We welcome blog post, pictures, and videos of you from your military experiences that you can share publicly or privatly.
Purpose

Our purpose is to bring the full military experience online. Lets be real, the military is everything but censored and other online platforms will not let us be our true, authentic crazy selves. Here at 22 - LIFE we encourage you to be exactly that!
What Seperates US

We pride ourselves on being different! We pride ourselves on being authentic! We bring the military straight to your fingertips! Most of all what seperates us from the rest, and what we pride ourselves the most on is our users ability to post annonomously. We know talking about your mental health post military as well as being active duty can feel like a heavy weight and unfortuantly 22 veteran a day tragically take their own lives. We are taking it upon ourselvesvs to do whatever we can to help bring that number down to...ZERO!
